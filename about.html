Delivering the ‘what’ when it comes to digital products.

I discover and solve problems using a combination of user research, data and UI design, making getting from point A to point B as easy as possible. I strive to make peoples’ lives easier.

How I approach creative problems

An open mind. Every problem has its own needs when finding a solution, so entering with an open mind helps me to look at the bigger picture and not tunnel vision on specific research practices.

When I hit a creative block, I tell myself it’s ok to park said problem for the time being and work on something else. Sometimes throwing ideas at a wall will never stick, all you need is some time away from the problem and you’ll come back with some glue.
